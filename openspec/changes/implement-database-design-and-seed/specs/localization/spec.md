## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Norwegian localization and currency

All UI labels, date, and currency formatting SHALL be Norwegian (nb_NO) and currency SHALL display NOK formatting for prices.

#### Scenario: Currency format

- **WHEN** prices are displayed in the UI
- **THEN** they SHALL be formatted as NOK with Norwegian locale conventions

#### Scenario: Norwegian text

- **WHEN** UI labels are shown
- **THEN** they SHALL be in Norwegian
